Privacy Policy for Talkify

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

Welcome to Talkify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Application includes:

A. Personal Data

  • Phone Number: Collected via Firebase Authentication to verify your identity and secure your account.
  • Profile Information: Optional data like display name or profile picture that you choose to provide.

B. Communications & Content

  • Chat Messages: Text messages sent within the app are processed and stored securely via Firebase Services to enable real-time messaging.
  • Video/Audio Calls: Real-time video and audio calls are powered by Agora.io. We do not record or store your video or audio calls on our servers.

C. Device and Usage Data

  • Device information (Model, OS version, unique device identifiers).
  • IP address and crash logs for performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Verify your mobile number and maintain your account.
  • Enable real-time chat functionality and peer-to-peer video calls.
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues.
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and enhance app security.

3. Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We use trusted third-party service providers to power our app features:

  • Google Firebase: Used for mobile number verification (SMS Authentication) and storing chat data. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Agora.io: Used to transmit real-time video and audio data for video calls. Agora acts as a data processor and does not store video streams. Agora Privacy Policy

4. Data Security & Retention

  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase to protect your data.
  • Retention: Chat histories are stored as long as your account is active. Video call data is temporary and streams are destroyed as soon as the call ends.

5. Permissions We Request

To provide our services, the app requires the following permissions on your device:

  • Camera: Required to initiate and receive video calls.
  • Microphone: Required for audio during video/voice calls.
  • SMS: Required for auto-reading the OTP verification code (optional).

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
